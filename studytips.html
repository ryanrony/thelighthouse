Study Tips

⏱️ Pomodoro Technique

Study in focused 50-minute intervals followed by 10-minute breaks. Helps maintain attention and prevents burnout.

📒 Flashcards, Flashcards, Flashcards

Test yourself regularly instead of just rereading notes. Pulling information from memory strengthens retention and helps you remember for longer.

🔥 Blurting

Review material and then spend 30-40 minutes simply writing down as much as you can remember without looking at the content. Then go back and see what you missed out to find any weakpoints.

📊 Habit Tracking

Split tasks between priority and how much time they take, being organised will take you from a D student to an A* student.

📵 Phone away during focus period

Keep screen time as a reward after at least 2 hours of study. It will make learning seem less boring. (If you are using the pomodoro method, don't use your phone during the 10 minute break, it will be really hard to get back to learning otherwise)

👑 The most important tip

Start RIGHT NOW, keeping it for later is a huge mistake. Don't regret it.
